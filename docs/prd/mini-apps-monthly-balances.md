# PRD: Mini-App - Monthly Balances

## Summary
Provide a monthly rollup of spending and net balance.

## Goals
- Summarize purchases per month.
- Show totals by category and spender.

## Non-Goals
- Advanced analytics or forecasting.

## User Stories
- As a user, I can view a month summary.
- As a user, I can switch months quickly.

## Functional Requirements
- Aggregate purchases by month.
- Filters by category and spender.
- Export summary as CSV.

## UX Notes
- Month selector with quick prev/next.

## Dependencies
- Spending data and categories.

## Milestones
1. Aggregation queries.
2. Summary view.
3. Filters and export.

## Risks
- Performance with large data sets.
